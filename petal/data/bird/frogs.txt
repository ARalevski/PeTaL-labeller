Lateral line placodes of aquatic vertebrates are evolutionarily conserved in mammals
<p><bold>Summary:</bold> Inhibition of apoptosis in mouse embryos reveals rudiments of the lateral line system, a sensory system common to fish and aquatic <strong><span style="color:yellowgreen">amphibia</span></strong>, but hypothesized to be completely lost in amniotes.</p>
