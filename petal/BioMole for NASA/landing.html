Above are the Ask Nature (asknature.org) functional groups belonging to the biomimicry taxonomy. When you enter the Ask Nature tool you can start exploring functional co-occurrence in the entire Ask Nature database.

  • Clicking angled text expands the groups into subgroups and functions.
  • You can explore any group, subgroup or function by adding a row (click on +) by brushing a circle across the length of the row.
  • Clicking a circle will give you a list of strategy pages that take you to Ask Nature.

All interactions with the tool are logged. By entering the tool, you acknowledge that you accept this.

Please enjoy!
